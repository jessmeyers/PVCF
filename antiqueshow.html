PVFC Annual Antique Show

The Pottersville Antique Show is a cherished annual event with a spirit of volunteerism unique to this special community.

Is is one of the East Coast’s oldest and most highly-regarded Antique Shows. You’ll find great bargains on useful and collectible items from 30 antiques dealers in the red Firehouse and the adjacent historic School House. Choose from pottery, textiles, prints, postcards, posters, jewelry of all kinds, porcelain, furniture, clocks, and vintage home furnishings.

Multiple generations of families and friends come to enjoy the wonderful food and special feeling of camaraderie, and many have been attending for as long as they can remember. Volunteers of all ages and walks of life are on hand to wait tables, peel peaches, scoop ice cream and serve delicious, home-style meals. And you can enjoy live music by local musicians along with your dinner.

All proceeds from the Pottersville Antique Show benefit the Pottersville Volunteer Fire Company. We thank The Ladies Auxiliary and our many friends who volunteer their time and energy to make the Pottersville Antique Show a success.
